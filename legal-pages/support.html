Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a license agreement?

Navigate to the track you want to license, tap "Generate License," fill in the licensee details and fee amount, then share the generated PDF. The license becomes binding when the licensee completes payment.

Are the license agreements legally binding?

Yes. Consigliary uses a payment-as-acceptance model that is compliant with the ESIGN Act and UETA. When a licensee pays for a license, their payment serves as their digital signature, making the agreement legally binding.

How do I upload music tracks?

Go to "My Tracks," tap the "+" button, and either select an audio file from your device or enter track metadata manually. The app supports common audio formats including MP3, WAV, and M4A.

What payment methods are accepted?

We use Stripe for payment processing, which accepts all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets including Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Can I manage split sheets and contributors?

Yes! For each track, you can add contributors, specify their roles, and set split percentages. The app ensures splits total 100% and helps you manage royalty distributions.

How is my data protected?

We use industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, bcrypt password hashing, and secure cloud storage. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Contact us at support@htdstudio.net to request a data export. We'll provide your data in a portable format within 30 days.
