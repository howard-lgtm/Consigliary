## üß™ What to Test

### **1. Onboarding Experience**

### **2. Dashboard & Navigation**
- [ ] Can you easily navigate between sections?

- [ ] Are the main features easy to find?
- [ ] Do the tab icons make sense?

**Test these sections:**
- Summary tab
- Activity tab
- Revenue tab
- Account tab

**Questions to consider:**
- Can you find what you need quickly?
- Is any section confusing or overwhelming?

---

### **3. Contract Analyzer**
- [ ] Upload a sample contract (PDF or image)
- [ ] Review the AI analysis results
- [ ] Check if key terms are highlighted correctly
- [ ] Test the risk assessment feature

**What to test:**
- Upload different contract types (recording, publishing, licensing)
- Try both PDF and image formats
- Check if analysis is accurate and helpful

**Questions to consider:**
- Does the analysis provide valuable insights?
- Are the recommendations clear and actionable?
- Would you trust this analysis for real contracts?

## üêõ Bug Reporting

If you encounter any issues, please report them with:

### **Critical Bugs** (App crashes, data loss, can't use features)
- What you were doing when it happened
- Steps to reproduce
- Device model and iOS version
- Screenshots if possible

### **Minor Issues** (UI glitches, typos, confusing elements)
- Where you saw the issue
- What you expected vs. what happened
- Screenshot if helpful

### **Feature Requests**
- What feature you'd like to see
- Why it would be valuable
- How you'd use it

## üìä Testing Scenarios

### **Scenario 1: New Artist**
You're an independent artist who just got offered your first record deal.
- Upload the contract to Contract Analyzer
- Review the AI analysis
- Check for red flags
- Make an informed decision

### **Scenario 2: Producer Collaboration**
You just finished a beat with 3 other producers.
- Create a split sheet
- Add all collaborators
- Set ownership percentages
- Generate and share the PDF

### **Scenario 3: Sync Licensing**
A TV show wants to license your song.
- Create a sync license agreement
- Customize the terms
- Generate the contract
- Send to the music supervisor

### **Scenario 4: Revenue Tracking**
You want to see how your music is performing financially.
- Check your revenue dashboard
- Review different income streams
- Analyze trends over time
- Plan your next moves
